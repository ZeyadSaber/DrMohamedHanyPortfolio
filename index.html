Bariatric surgery is just the beginning, To avoid all probable obesity surgery side effects, such as hair loss and Anemia, our nutritionists are here to provide you with tips and diet to keep up being healthy and fit.

PSYCHIATRIST

A patient’s psychological state is one of the main obstacles before and after the surgery, our psychiatrists will help you reach the right psychological state after the surgery

INTERNISTS

Diabetes and hypertension are major problems that can obstruct the way into a healthy transformation. At Dr. Mohamed Hany Ashour Bariatric Center there’s a professional group of internists who will be with you in every step you take whether before, during or after your bariatric surgery. Providing you with the pre and post surgery check up to avoid any distortion in your health!

ENDOCRINOLOGISTS

Your Endocrinologist will be with you all along your transformation journey to diagnose and treat conditions like diabetes, thyroid diseases, metabolic disorders, and disorders in the hormone-producing adrenal glands.

DERMATOLOGISTS

Dermatologists will add the final touch to your transformation journey. They’ll help you to get rid of the problems that occur after losing weight, such as Skin laxity, pigmentations, and hair loss. Helping you to complete the best picture of yourself!
